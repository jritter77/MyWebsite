Game Design

I have always greatly enjoyed video games, and got interested in trying to develop one back in high-school. I started using a program called GameMaker, which ultimately led to my interest in programming and to this point in my life. Unfortunately I have yet to finish any games or officially release any, however I have developed countless demos of different types of games, and have been working on a project now for a while I hope to release in the near future. The game on this page is a simple arcade style rail shooter I made a couple of years ago, it is endless so the only point is to really see how high of a score you can get.

Snowboarding

I started snowboarding back when I was about 12 or 13 years old and instantly fell in love with it. I try to go at least a few times every year, though this has been more difficult since I have moved to the coast. I typically go to Mt. Shasta which isn't too far, and I can stop and visit with my friends and family in Weaverville along the way. The video on this page is from a trip I took to Shasta last season with my younger sister and her boyfriend.

Travel Abroad

I love to travel to distant places and explore other countries. Sadly the current state of the world makes that extremely difficult. A few years ago I spent 3 weeks in Japan on a solo backpacking trip in which adventured around the country primarily by train and stayed in hostels. It was an amazing journey full of great times, people and experiences. I definitely plan to visit again someday! The pictures displayed on this page are from when I was in Japan. The video is from a week-long festival in Aomori in which they construct massive paper lantern floats that typically celebrate traditional dieties ad folklore.
